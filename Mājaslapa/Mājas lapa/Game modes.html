What is this game and what is the game point?

This is game is about black friday - fighting for all in the shop.

Little story of the game - Man, who just woke up turned on television while eating breakfast, there he saw that during the day is going to be black friday sale.

The shop was realy big and the strangest thing was that the buyers were not as much as he imagined and that the store was demolished
